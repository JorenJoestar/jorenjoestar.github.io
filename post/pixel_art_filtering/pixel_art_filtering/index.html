Pixel Art Filtering

2021-04-12 1 min read

Pixel art is living a beautiful renaissance these last years, with more and more studios embracing its visuals and more artists going back to this unique way of describing visuals in a game. With that comes also some problems that arise from the use of modern GPUs that are radically different than older consoles, especially for dynamic effects like scaling.
